My Travel Journey

My name is OJ Shin. I love travel. Traveling around and exploring new places is one of best things you can. Are you ready to go my travel journey with me? Every place is great and fun!!

  • Toronto, Canada
  • East-South Coast, U.S.A

1. Fukuoka, Japan

Japan. Fukuoka 2003 December We took ship instead of airplane. Japan looks similar my country, but different culture and environment. It located more lower latitude than my country. So weather and spices of plant were new to me.

2. Toronto, Canada

Canada Toronto 2004 August When I came in USA, I stopped by in Canada, Toronto. It was my first backpacking trip by myself. No plan, just finding new things... It was awesome experience.

3. East-South Coast, U.S.A

Amtrak traveling east and south coast 20 days. Took 30days pass east and south coast Amtrak pass on 2005 December It makes me, I am in Amerian Frontier. I was in US transcontinental railroad. I just saw movies and read a book about a transcontinental railway. Moving state to state by train, it was awesome expirence. It took so long to go each states but view was fantastic especially going from Washington D.C to Miami south beach.

4. Colorado, CO

Colorado. 2008 summer It is one of my favorite place. Rockies mountain is so beautiful and amazing God's place.

5. Santa Monica, CA

Santa Monica 2014 February When my husband got a first decent job, Company sent to him in Santa Monica,CA for conference. We went together with my first girl. It was our first worry-free traveling. Because we've been so long beaten down by life, during 9 years, which was my husband student journey . That traveling gave us some sense of relief. We still have financially and emotionally struggle, but not that time. One of my happiest moment

Pensacola-Florida 2015 February Pensacola! When Eliana was 15 months, we went Pensacola, FL. Physically, she saw the wave and sea first time her life, since she recognized things. We spent very precious time all together. We loves this picture!!!
